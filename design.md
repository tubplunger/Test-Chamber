# Test-Chamber
2D Unity game with inspiration from Vampire Survivors though with a sci-fi theming.

With this being a 2D game movement is going to be limited in going up and down, and left and right. For player attacks there are going to be different weapons that have different ranges and all that, but the player
will use the mouse to aim where they want and click down to fire the attack.

As with a game like this there are going to be different enemy types. Within the game you are going to be fighting robots, starting off with simple ones that just move at the player and cause damage upon melee. 
We'll also get some ranged enemies in and maybe a variety of both that have higher health and tougher attacks. Also, possibly a miniboss enemy that can take a lot of fire.

For spawning, the enemies will spawn at the edge of the arena and waves will work that a certain number and type of enemies will be sent out and when they are defeated the next wave will come in with more enemies.
The arena is meant to be mostly be accessible the entire time, so if the player ends up near one of the edges where they spawn the spawners will stop working until the player moves out of range.

Start --> Wave --> Victory/Game Over
